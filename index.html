a letter · from your baby · with all my love 🖤

My dearest Didu,

I know right now you might be angry or hurt because of me, and I completely understand. I hate that I made you feel this way.

You are not just my elder sister. You are my Didu — the one who has always taken care of me, who still calls me "Baby" and "Baccha" with so much affection, who scolds me when I’m wrong but loves me even more after that. You have been my protector, my guide, and my safe place since I was little.

I’m truly, deeply sorry for what I did. I never wanted to hurt you. I was thoughtless and I let my anger or ego get the better of me. Seeing you upset because of me is breaking my heart. You deserve only love, respect, and happiness from your little brother, and I failed you this time.

You have always been there for me — through my tantrums, my silly fights, my bad days, and my late-night talks. You listen to me, you guide me, and you love me unconditionally. And instead of cherishing that, I hurt the one person who loves me the most in the family.

Please forgive me, Didu. I promise I will be a better brother from today. I will think before I speak, I will control my temper, and I will never take your love for granted again. I want to make you proud and make you smile, not make you sad.

You are my big sister, my role model, and one of the most important people in my life. I can’t imagine growing up without your love and care. Please give your "Baccha" one more chance. I miss your smile and your warm words already.

I love you so much, Didu. More than words can ever say. Please forgive me.

with all my regret, guilt, and endless love, Your Baby / Baccha
